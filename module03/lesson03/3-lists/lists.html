There are some devices commonly used in various company structures, which are:

Switch
Device used in computer networks to forward frames between different nodes, with each port corresponding to a different collision domain.
HUB
Device used in computer networks to forward frames between different nodes, with all ports corresponding to the same collision domain.
Router
Device responsible for forwarding communication packets to their recipients within a network or between networks, selecting the most efficient route available at the time.
